About

Hello everyone! My name's Tim.

I'm a Graduate Developer at Building Blocks based in rainy Manchester, UK. At work, I predominantly work with Microsoft's ASP.NET MVC 4.5 and SDL Tridion 2013. Outside of work, I enjoy playing with Android development and dabble with a Raspberry Pi.
