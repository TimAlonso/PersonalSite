About

Hello everyone! My name's Tim.

I'm a Graduate Developer at Building Blocks based in rainy Manchester, UK. At work, I predominantly work with the Microsoft's ASP.NET MVC 4.5 and SDL Tridion 2013. Outside of work, I enjoy playing with Android development and dabble with a Raspberry Pi.

When I can afford it, I enjoy travelling. I hope to visit Italy, South America and South East Asia at some point before I reach the tender age of 30.
